How to Make Fried Rice

About Documentation

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Mushroom Broth
  • Cabbage
  • Garlic
  • Red Onion
  • Fried Rice Seasoning

Tools

  • Pan
  • Spoon
  • Plate

Steps

  1. Fry the rice by lightly browning in a pan
  2. Stir-fry the garlic,red onion,cabbage until tender
  3. Make a large well in the center of the pan with the rice
  4. Pour in the whisked eggs and scramble until small curds form
  5. Sauce is optional but adds a savory flavor to the rice
  6. Add salt and mushroom broth in a pan
  7. Add 1 sachet of fried rice seasoning
  8. Stir fried rice until ripe
